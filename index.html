Fotune Top 30 Companies Performance Comparison in Year 2020

In 2020 Covid-19 hit the world, econmy slowed down, companies preformed quite different. Financial market droped sharply and quickly recovered, retailing companies had good growth since people are in demand of daily supplies. Fortune top 30 companies in 2020 are compared in this visualization in three different scenes by revenue, by market cap and by profit. Companies are color coded based on their industry, details like company location, number of employee and %diff compared with other companies can be obtained by interacting with the chart.
